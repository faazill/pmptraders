Product Description

Presenting the Aqua 2090 RO+Alkaline Water Purifier, a state-of-the-art water filtration system designed to provide pure and healthy water for your home or office. In a sleek Premium Metallic Blue finish, this 10-liter purifier combines RO and Alkaline technology to ensure your water is safe, healthy, and tastes just right.

With advanced purification systems, this purifier not only removes harmful contaminants but also enriches your water with essential minerals, promoting better health and well-being.

Why Aqua 2090 is the Ultimate Water Purifier:

The Aqua 2090 is designed with multiple stages of purification to provide you with the cleanest water possible. Using RO filtration, it removes dissolved impurities, heavy metals, and toxins from your water, ensuring that the water you drink is free from harmful contaminants.

The alkaline filter adds essential minerals like calcium and magnesium to support overall health, while maintaining the perfect pH balance for optimal hydration.

Return Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Return Policy Overview

At PMP Traders, we stand behind the quality of our products. We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for manufacturing defects only. Our return policy is designed to ensure customer satisfaction while maintaining fair business practices.

Important: Refund or replacement only for manufacturing defect. This policy applies to defects that are present at the time of manufacturing and are not caused by improper use, installation, or maintenance by the customer.

2. What Can Be Returned

2.1 Eligible for Return

  • Products with manufacturing defects
  • Products that arrive damaged during shipping
  • Products that don't match the description
  • Products with missing parts or components

2.2 Return Conditions

To be eligible for return, products must meet the following criteria:

  • Return request made within 7 days of delivery
  • Product in original packaging
  • Product unused and in original condition
  • All original accessories and components included
  • Clear evidence of manufacturing defect

3. What Cannot Be Returned

Non-Returnable Items:
  • Used Products: Products showing signs of use, installation, or wear
  • Damaged Products: Products damaged due to customer handling or installation
  • Missing Packaging: Products without original packaging or accessories
  • Consumable Items: Filters, membranes, cartridges, and other consumable parts
  • Installation Services: Installation charges are non-refundable once service is provided
  • Custom Orders: Products ordered with specific customizations
  • Time Expired: Products purchased more than 7 days ago

4. Return Process

How to Return a Product:

  1. Contact Us: Call our customer support at +91 9946170056 or +91 9656622617 within 7 days of delivery
  2. Describe the Issue: Explain the manufacturing defect or problem you're experiencing
  3. Get Approval: Our team will review your request and provide return authorization if eligible
  4. Package Product: Securely package the product in its original packaging with all accessories
  5. Ship Back: We will arrange pickup or provide shipping instructions
  6. Inspection: Our team will inspect the returned product
  7. Refund/Replacement: Upon approval, we'll process your refund or send a replacement

5. Refund Process

5.1 Refund Timeline

  • Processing Time: 2-3 business days after receiving the returned product
  • Bank Processing: 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account
  • Total Time: 5-8 business days from receiving the return

5.2 Refund Method

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method used for the purchase:

  • Credit/Debit cards: Refunded to the same card
  • UPI: Refunded to the same UPI ID
  • Net Banking: Refunded to the same bank account
  • Cash on Delivery: Bank transfer to your account

5.3 Refund Amount

  • Full product price for manufacturing defects
  • Shipping charges refunded if return is due to our error
  • Installation charges are non-refundable

6. Replacement Policy

6.1 When Replacement is Offered

  • Manufacturing defects confirmed by our team
  • Product under warranty period
  • Same product is available in stock

6.2 Replacement Process

  • We will ship the replacement product within 3-5 business days
  • Free shipping for replacement products
  • Original product must be returned before replacement is shipped
  • Replacement warranty starts from the date of replacement delivery

7. Warranty Information

7.1 Warranty Coverage

All products come with manufacturer warranty as specified in product descriptions:

  • Pump and SMPS: 1 year warranty
  • Installation: Provided by seller (charges apply: ₹500-700)
  • Consumable Parts: Not covered under warranty

7.2 What's Covered

  • Manufacturing defects in motor (pump)
  • Manufacturing defects in SMPS
  • Defects in electrical components

7.3 What's Not Covered

  • Plastic and glass body damage
  • Consumable items (sediment, carbon, membranes, etc.)
  • Improper installation by customer
  • Damage due to misuse or accidents
  • Normal wear and tear

8. Shipping and Handling

8.1 Return Shipping

  • Free return shipping for manufacturing defects
  • Customer pays shipping for non-defect returns
  • We arrange pickup for eligible returns

8.2 Packaging Requirements

  • Product must be in original packaging
  • All accessories and manuals included
  • Proper protection to prevent damage during transit
  • Return authorization number clearly marked on package
